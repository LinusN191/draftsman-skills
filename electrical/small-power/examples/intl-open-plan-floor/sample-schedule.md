## Notes

- All workstation Schuko sockets are floor-box mounted (mount=floor, 0 mm AFFL) — typical INT open-plan office practice with raised access floor or floor cores. Each floor box hosts 2× Schuko (counted as a single double socket each) + structured data.
- Meeting-room low-level sockets at 300 mm AFFL on wall; high-level AV socket at 1200 mm AFFL on rear wall (for ceiling projector or wall-mounted display feed).
- Kitchenette worktop sockets at 1100 mm AFFL (150 mm above 950 mm worktop); under-counter fridge fed via 16 A FCU at 300 mm AFFL on C05.
- Server-room small-power sockets at 600 mm AFFL on wall behind rack; UPS-fed; **Type B 30 mA RCD** on circuit C06 per `IEC 60364-5-53:2002+A2:2015 §531.3.3`. This citation form is exactly identical to the `db-layout/intl-dbcomms-data` shipped policy at db-layout v1.3.0+ — cross-skill alignment by engineering convention (small-power v1.0 is a leaf skill with no upstream intent consumption).
- Toilet is classified `bathroom_zone_3` per IEC 60364-7-701:2019 §701.512; only the BS EN 61558-2-5 shaver supply unit is installed (no general Schuko within 3 m of zone 1 boundary).
- Outdoor sockets in IP65 weatherproof enclosure (BS EN 60529 + IEC 60364-5-53:2002+A2:2015 §512.2) at 1200 mm AFFL on external wall — smoking shelter and bin store.
- All eight circuits are radial (none ring) — IEC 60364 has no ring-final equivalent to BS 7671 §433.1.5; INV-04 satisfied trivially under INT.
- All MCBs are 10 kA Icu to match the declared utility PSCC at the parent DB-FLOOR-01 busbar per IEC 60364-4-43:2017 §434.5.1 + IEC 60898-1.
- RCD protection at DB-FLOOR-01 is implemented as **three banks**: Bank A (Type A 30 mA) covers C01-C05; Bank B (**Type B 30 mA**) covers C06 only; Bank C (Type A 30 mA) covers C07 + C08. Bank B is segregated so the Type B device sits upstream of only the server-room circuit, avoiding any nuisance-trip coordination conflict with downstream Type A devices.
- All circuits carry `tool_call_pending_for_zs_verification: true`; loop-impedance Zs delegated to `calc.zs_loop_impedance` per WI3.
- Cable lengths quoted are total radial length and assume IEC 60364-5-52:2009+A1:2019 Annex A reference methods (raised access floor for workstation zones; ceiling void for meeting rooms + kitchenette; external wall containment for C08); final route by `cable-containment` skill.
