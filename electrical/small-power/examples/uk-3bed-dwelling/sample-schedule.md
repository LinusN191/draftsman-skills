## Notes

- All sockets BS 1363 double 13A switched (UK standard) unless explicitly noted.
- Mounting heights AFFL (above finished floor level): general 450 mm per Approved Document M; above worktop 1150 mm (= 150 mm above 1000 mm worktop); cooker CCU 1350 mm; outdoor 1100 mm.
- Bathroom (Part 7-701) is classified `bathroom_zone_3`; only the BS EN 61558-2-5 shaver supply unit is installed. No BS 1363 sockets within 3 m of zone 1 (§701.512.3).
- Outdoor socket fed by 13A FCU spur from the ground-floor ring per BS 7671 §522.6.201; IP65 enclosure; additional 30 mA RCD protection by virtue of upstream RCBO (§522.6.202).
- All circuits carry `tool_call_pending_for_zs_verification: true`; loop-impedance Zs delegated to `calc.zs_loop_impedance` per WI3.
- Cable lengths quoted are *total ring length* (not half-leg) and assume Reference Method 100/101 (clipped direct in stud/under floorboards); final route by `cable-containment` skill.
