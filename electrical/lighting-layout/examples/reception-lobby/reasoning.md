# Example: Reception Lobby — Reasoning

Demonstrates: mixed CCT selection (3000K warm for lobby ambience),
CRI 90 for welcoming entrance, Part L perimeter zone with north glazing,
entrance transition zone luminance ratios per BS 8300.

## Key notes:
- Em = 300 lux (lobby per BS EN 12464-1:2021 Table 5.3)
- 3000K CCT appropriate for hospitality/reception — warm welcome tone
- North glazing: daylight linking required in Z1 perimeter zone (2000mm from north wall)
- BS 8300 entrance transition: luminance step ratio must not exceed 10:1 exterior-to-interior
