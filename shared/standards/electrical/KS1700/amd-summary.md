# KS 1700:2018 — Amendment History

KS 1700:2018 is the first published edition of the Kenyan wiring code.

## Edition history

| Edition | Published | Effective | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| KS 1700:2018 First Edition | 2018-09-21 | 2019-01-01 | Current |

## No formal amendments to date

As of 2026-05-18, KS 1700:2018 has not been formally amended. KEBS practice is to bundle amendments into a revised edition rather than published amendment slips.

## Planned revision cycle

Per KEBS publishing policy, KS standards are reviewed on a 5-year cycle. KS 1700:2018 is therefore expected to be revised c. 2024 (or later if delayed). The next edition will likely incorporate:

- Native §722 EV charging clauses (currently deferred to IEC 60364-7-722)
- Solar PV / battery storage updates aligned with IEC 60364-7-712 / 7-826
- Smart meter and AMI provisions for prosumer installations
- Updated ambient temperature baselines per latest climate data

## Cross-references to BS 7671 amendments

Because KS 1700 adopts BS 7671 substantially, BS 7671 amendments not yet reflected in KS 1700 are:

- **BS 7671 AMD 1 (2020)** — AFDD requirements (not yet in KS 1700:2018)
- **BS 7671 AMD 2 (2022)** — EV charging §722 (KS routes to IEC 60364-7-722 instead); overvoltage clarifications

These can be applied as informative guidance under KS 1700 §1.6 (engineering judgement) until the next KS edition incorporates them formally.
