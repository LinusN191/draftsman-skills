# ISO 19650 — Key Terminology

**Source:** ISO 19650-1:2018 §3 (Terms and definitions). Largely aligned with BS 1192:2007+A2:2016 §3.

## Roles and parties

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Appointing party** | Receiver of services / asset owner (formerly "Client" or "Employer") |
| **Lead appointed party** | Main consultant or contractor delivering services |
| **Appointed party** | Sub-consultant or sub-contractor working under the lead appointed party |
| **Delivery team** | Collective term for the lead + appointed parties |
| **Task team** | Single discipline team within a delivery team |
| **Information Manager** | Person responsible for information management on a project |

## Information requirements

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **EIR** | Exchange Information Requirements — pre-appointment requirements set by the appointing party |
| **AIR** | Asset Information Requirements — what's needed for the operational phase |
| **PIR** | Project Information Requirements — what's needed during the delivery phase |
| **OIR** | Organizational Information Requirements — strategic requirements of the appointing party |
| **BEP** | BIM Execution Plan — how the delivery team will meet the EIR |
| **MIDP** | Master Information Delivery Plan |
| **TIDP** | Task Information Delivery Plan per task team |

## Information models

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **PIM** | Project Information Model — information model during the delivery phase |
| **AIM** | Asset Information Model — information model during the operational phase |
